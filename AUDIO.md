# Audio sources and licenses

No recorded audio files are shipped in this iteration.

The optional ambience control uses Web Audio oscillators generated locally in the browser after an explicit user action. It creates separate gain channels named ambience, fire, crowd and narration. The narration channel is silent until a future properly licensed narration asset is added.

Because no third-party recording is distributed, there is currently no external audio license or attribution requirement. The complete visual and educational experience remains available when Web Audio is unavailable or disabled.
